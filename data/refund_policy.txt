# Refund and Return Policy

## 200% Money-Back Guarantee (Twice Money Pay)
We stand behind the absolute quality and authenticity of our products. If you receive an item that is proven to be counterfeit, fundamentally defective right out of the box, or severely completely different from what was advertised, you are eligible for our "Twice Money Pay" guarantee. 
Under this strict policy, if your claim is verified, we will refund your original purchase price in full to your payment method, AND we will issue an additional 100% of the item's value in store credit (giving you a 200% total refund). To claim this, you must contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery with clear photographic evidence. Regular returns for sizing or "change of mind" do not qualify for this policy.

## General Return Window
Customers can return items within 30 days of the original purchase date. The items must be in their original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags and packaging intact.

## Extended Holiday Returns
Purchases made between November 1st and December 24th are eligible for our extended holiday return window. These items can be returned for a full refund until January 31st of the following year.

## Non-Refundable Items
The following items are strictly non-refundable and are considered final sale:
- Digital gift cards
- Downloadable software products
- Customized or personalized items
- Clearance items marked as "Final Sale"
- Intimates, swimwear, and pierced jewelry (for hygiene reasons)

## Process for Refunds
To initiate a refund, please follow these steps:
1. Log in to your account and navigate to "Order History".
2. Select the order and click "Request Refund".
3. Select the reason for the return from the dropdown menu.
4. Print the provided pre-paid return label and attach it to the package.
5. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping center.

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, it takes 3-5 business days to process the refund to the original payment method.

## Return Shipping Costs
If the return is due to our error (e.g., you received an incorrect or defective item), we will cover the return shipping costs. For all other returns (e.g., "changed my mind" or "wrong size"), a flat $5.99 return shipping fee will be deducted from your final refund amount.

## Exchanges
We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or color, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

## Damaged or Defective Items
If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery. Please include your order number and clear photos of the damage. We will immediately issue a replacement or a full refund without requiring a standard return.

## Late or Missing Refunds
If you haven’t received a refund within 7 business days of our confirmation email, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact our support team.
